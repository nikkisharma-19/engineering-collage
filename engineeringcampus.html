Engineering College or Institutes in Sikar


Arise, awake, and stop not till the goal is reached……. Swami Vivekananda
Leaders do something differently but winners do things which are strategically different, which give them competitive advantage. The objective of Ranked AA+ Shekhawati Group of Institutions is to provide the best education which will enable young countrymen, boys and girls to built the carries as professionals managers, to equip them with the latest concept and upto date knowledge to develop in the managerial skills and aptitude, to instill business ethics and thus to roll out competent managers to perform superbly. Shekhawati Group of Institutions have been promoted by the Gurukul Shikshan Sansthan a non-profit organization founded in 2002, in Sikar. The organization is dedicated to promote professionals and technical education in the state and is presently conducting various programmers at undergraduate and post graduate levels from Institutions. The Programmers are approved by AICTE New Delhi, NCTE, PCI, BCI, RPMC, RCI, GoR, NCVT and affiliated to BTU Bikaner, PDU Shekhawati University Sikar, RUHS, RAJUVAS, DARRAU Jodhpur, BTER, Jodhpur, SKNAU Jobner.
